FELIPE FRONCHETTI
Researcher in Software Engineering São Paulo, Brazil University of São Paulo fronchetti@usp.br
SUMMARY
Fronchetti is a Master's Degree student in Computer Science at the University of São Paulo (USP), Brazil. His research lies in the intersection between Software Engineering (SE) and Computer Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW). He is co-(author) of 8 papers in top tier journals and conferences on Software Engineering, working on the supporting of newcomers in Open Source Software communities. He also received the Best Paper Award in the VEM 2017 Workshop.
